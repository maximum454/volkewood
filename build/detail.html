About uv poly finished hardwoods

The French Oak Collection comprises of solid and engineered hardwood floors, all hand- finished by skilled artisans. We offer a wide range of plank wood with custom sizes, colors, and textures that create our coveted collection. The unique character of each plank is achieved by implementing a variety of techniques to obtain particular textures and patinas. This wood has been lightly fumed for a distinct and sophisticated look with its depth of color and tone.

  • Surface Texture: Lightly Brushed
  • Open Grains: Yes
  • Knots: Yes
  • Knots Size: Small to Large
  • Open Knots: Filled
  • Color Range/Variation: Low/Medium
  • Made to Look Reclaimed: No
  • Tumbled: No
  • Edge Profile: Smooth, Eased
  • Prefinished Color From Poly: Yes
  • Prefinished Color From Fumed**: Yes
  • Can Wood Be Stained After Installation: No
  • Can Wood Be Resanded: Yes 2-3 times
